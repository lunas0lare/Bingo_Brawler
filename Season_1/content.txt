			<h1>Format</h1>
			<div class="text">
				<p>
					The league format will be lockout 1v1’s. The first half of the league will be a Round robin setup where each player gets to play against each other once, resulting in 7 matches for each individual. After that phase is completed the top 4 players will be moved into a Single Elimination best of 3’s tournament bracket for 1v1 lockout.
				</p>
				<h2>What is Lockout?</h2>
				<p>
					Players try to complete a single line (5 squares in a row on any row/column/diagonal) as quickly as possible. Squares are locked out by a player claiming them; if Player A claims a square, Player B can no longer claim that square.
					<br>
					The first player to complete a line wins. If a bingo line win condition is not possible for one player, the opponent can win by majority. A Bingo line is a priority win over majority. If at the end of 2 hours there isnt a winner (By bingo or majority (13 squares)), whoever has the most squares wins. A tie is only possible in the round robin matches if the timer of 2 hours runs out and both players have the same amount of squares.
				</p>
				<p>
					Want to try it out yourself and with your friends? The JSON file with all the objectives is down below!
				</p>

				<h2>Bingo Board</h2>
				<p>
					Winning lines are horizontal, vertical and diagonal. If no winning lines are available, majority wins.

							<li>Skipping bosses unintended to be skipped is banned (e.g. skipping Godskin Noble to get to Rykard)</li>
							<li>Gravity kill on Radahn is banned</li>
							<li>Gravity kills on Night’s Cavalry and other minor bosses are allowed</li>
							<li>Quitouts are allowed</li>

						<ul>
							<li>For squares regarding boss kills, please wait for the “Enemy Felled” text to appear before marking</li>
						</ul>
					</li>
					<li>For squares that mention “bosses”, enemies must have a red health bar at the bottom of the screen to count (e.g. Greyoll does not count towards “Defeat 3 dragon bosses”)</li>

					<li>For squares that mention “Caves/Grottos”, “Catacombs”, or “Tunnels”, only dungeons that specifically contain those words in their title count (e.g. “Ruin-Strewn Precipice” does not count as a cave, but “Cave of Knowledge” does)</li>

							<li>Mt. Gelmir counts as Altus Plateau</li>

							<li>Grafted Scion counts towards Limgrave bosses</li>
						</ul>
					</li>
